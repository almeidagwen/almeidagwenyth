First Steps

  • First, in a medium bowl, whisk together the flours, baking soda, baking powder, cornstarch, and salt. Set aside.
  • In a separate bowl, beat the butter and sugars with a mixer on medium speed for 2 minutes. Add the egg and the vanilla, beat until smooth.
